When Hamas Militants FaceTimed Israeli Hostages' Families, the Mutual Dehumanization Cracked

On the morning of Monday, October 13, the mobile phones of family members of the hostages rang. Hamas members were on the other end of the line. Einav Zangauker, Julie Kupershtein, Ruhama Bohbot and other parents, who had waited for hundreds of days, got a video call from their loved ones. Not through the Red Cross or mediators, but a direct call from Hamas to the families.
