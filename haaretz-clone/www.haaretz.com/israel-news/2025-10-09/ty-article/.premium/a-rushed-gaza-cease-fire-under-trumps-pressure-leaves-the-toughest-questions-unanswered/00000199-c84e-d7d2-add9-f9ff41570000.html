A Rushed Gaza Cease-fire Under Trump's Pressure Leaves the Toughest Questions Unanswered

The cease-fire halts the war and frees hostages, yet with no roadmap for Gaza's future – its borders, leadership or reconstruction – it risks becoming another short-lived lull in a conflict with no end in sight

The dramatic announcement of a cease-fire agreement in the Gaza Strip came after two days of intensive talks in Sharm el-Sheikh. Until late Wednesday, sources involved in the negotiations insisted that nothing had been finalized. Senior Palestinian officials familiar with the talks said that "all issues are still on the table," and it was too early to speak of a final agreement.
